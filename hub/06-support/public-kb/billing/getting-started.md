# Billing Getting Started

## What This Guide Covers

This guide explains the basic billing flow for customers using the Alfafaa platform.

## Before You Start

- make sure your account is active
- confirm that a valid payment method is attached
- confirm that your subscription plan is assigned

## First Billing Cycle

1. Your workspace is activated.
2. The platform creates the initial billing schedule.
3. The first successful charge produces an invoice record.

## If Payment Fails

- the system retries based on the configured recovery window
- your account owner may receive a notification
- service restrictions depend on your billing policy

## Need Help

Contact support with:

- workspace id
- invoice id if available
- the time of the failed attempt
