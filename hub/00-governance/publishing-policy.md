# Publishing Policy

## Auto-Publish

Auto-publish is allowed when:

- `publish: true`
- `status: approved` or `status: active`
- `visibility` is not `restricted`
- required metadata is present

## Approval Required

Approval is required when:

- `visibility: restricted`
- document includes sensitive security or compliance details
- document changes external guidance in a regulated or high-risk area

## Publish Targets

- `public-site`
- `internal-site`
- `engineering-site`
